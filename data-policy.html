Delete Your Mafia Underworlds Account

To delete your account and all associated data, please email us at: support@mafiaunderworlds.com with your username or email address used in the app.

We will delete all your account data including your username, email, progress, and other associated data within 7 days of receiving your request.

Data Deleted:

  • Username and email
  • Game progress
  • Stored preferences

Data Retained:

  • Anonymous crash logs (used for diagnostics)

We will keep no personal data beyond 7 days after the deletion request.
