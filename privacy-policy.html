Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2024

Mafia Underworlds is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our game.

1. Information We Collect

  • Basic device and account identifiers (e.g., Device ID, platform, language, country).
  • Gameplay data (progress, scores, preferences).
  • Optional email address for support purposes.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To improve and personalize your gaming experience.
  • To analyze gameplay metrics and performance.
  • To communicate with you (e.g., support, updates).

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. We may share data with service providers (e.g., analytics, crash reporting) solely to operate and improve the game.

4. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

5. Children's Privacy

The game is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13 years of age.

6. Your Rights

You can request deletion of your data or contact us for questions at support@mafiaunderworlds.com.

7. Updates

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here and in-game where appropriate.

Thank you for playing Mafia Underworlds.
